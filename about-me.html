about me


Hey there, I'm Catherine! Sometimes I'm an anglerfish. Most of the time, however, I'm a person.

I study digital media art at San Jose State University! Personally, I really like using technology with art. I intially majored in computer science, but found that sorting data by algorithms was terribly boring and not as engaging as I would've liked. Digital media art really forged the bridge between my artistic and technical sides and it's the kind of thing that inspires me, since there's so many directions I could take this.

As an artist, I'm story-driven. In my spare time, I enjoy writing stories, developing narrative-driven games and driving myself insane from all the ideas I have yet to implement.
