about me


Hey there, I'm Catherine! Sometimes I'm an anglerfish. Most of the time, however, I'm a person.

I study digital media art at San Jose State University! Despite intially majoring in computer science, I found the subject to be dry and didn't fulfil me as creatively as I had hoped. See, I did enjoy the coding aspect of it, but when it was mostly about sorting data, that was where it lost me since there was a lot less room to mess around. Digital media art was a good fusion of what I liked, so here I am.

In my spare time, I enjoy drawing, developing narrative-driven games and driving myself insane from all the ideas I have yet to implement.
